## Welcome to Embedded Systems II

Welcome to the new public website for Embedded Systems II (EEE3096/5S). This website is still partly under construction and will be fully operational within in the first week of term. There are two course codes linked to this course, namely EEE3096 (for Electrical Engineering programmes) and EEE3095S (for Computer Science programmes and is also available to students in other Science faculty programmes that adequately satisfy the prerequisites).

The main objective of this course is taking your initial understanding of embedded systems and development of code for embedded systems further, extending both your theoretical understanding of embedded systems in the lectures, and your practical application and understanding of effective development practices during the lab pracs.
